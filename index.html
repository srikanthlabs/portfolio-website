01 — Who am I

From the pharmacy bench to the media dashboard.

I spent four years training in pharmaceutical science before I ever touched an ads manager — a Bachelor of Pharmacy, 8.63 CGPA, built on controlled variables, protocol, and reading data for what it actually says rather than what you want it to say.

That discipline is the whole reason paid social clicked for me. A campaign is just another system with inputs, variables and a measurable output. I moved into performance marketing to run that system at scale — full-funnel strategy across five platforms, managing budgets that don't forgive guesswork.

Today I sit inside Omnicom's global network handling 12+ international and EMEA accounts, but the instinct hasn't changed: watch the signal, not the noise, and never let a campaign drift more than you can explain.

The pivot, honestly
Why a pharmacist runs paid media

Pharma taught me to distrust anecdotes and trust the protocol. Paid social rewarded that instinct immediately — pacing accuracy, statistical significance in creative testing, and reading a dashboard the way you'd read a lab result: calmly, and before you react. It's a stranger career path than most in this industry, and it's exactly why I optimize differently.

Personal case studyEMEA multi-account portfolio · Omnicom Global Solutions
Problem

Reporting overhead was eating strategy time

Twelve-plus EMEA accounts meant twelve-plus manual reporting cycles. Strategic thinking time was getting crowded out by pulling numbers.

Strategy

Automate the pull, protect the analysis

Built 8+ automated Looker Studio and Power BI dashboards feeding directly from platform data sources, standardized across account types so account leads could self-serve.

Execution

Pacing QA + tiered reporting cadence

Layered weekly pacing checks (±3% target) with monthly, PCA and end-of-campaign reports — each tier answering a different stakeholder question rather than repeating the same numbers.

Result

Time back, trust up

6+ hours per week returned to strategic work across the team, alongside a 92% client retention rate and an 18% lift in conversion rates from the freed-up optimization time.
